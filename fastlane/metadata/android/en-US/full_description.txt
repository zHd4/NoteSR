NoteSR is a privacy-first, offline note app that keeps your data on your device.

Create text notes, attach files, and open attachments directly in the app, all protected with AES-256 encryption. No account, no cloud sync, and no tracking.

<ul>
    <li>Encrypted local storage for notes and attachments</li>
    <li>Fully offline usage (no internet required)</li>
    <li>Built-in viewer for common file types</li>
    <li>Encrypted export/import for backup and restore</li>
    <li>Simple, minimal interface focused on privacy</li>
</ul>

NoteSR is designed for people who want secure personal notes, journals, and sensitive files without giving up control of their data.
