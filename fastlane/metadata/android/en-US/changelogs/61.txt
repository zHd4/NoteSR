Improvements:

• Enhanced data safety with a new transactional system that prevents data loss or corruption if the app is closed or crashes during important tasks.
• Improved file handling by adopting the standard Android system file picker for all backup and import operations.
• Reduced app requirements by removing unnecessary network-related permissions.
• Significant boost in overall app stability through a complete overhaul of how background services are managed.

Bug Fixes:

• Resolved a critical issue that prevented users on Android 10 from successfully saving backups.
• Fixed a potential issue that could cause data corruption if a storage re-encryption process was interrupted.
• Resolved an issue where errors during re-encryption process were not properly communicated to the user.
